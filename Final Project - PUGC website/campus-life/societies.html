Societies

University of the Punjab, Gujranwala Campus offers a variety of student societies and clubs to enhance the overall campus experience. These societies provide opportunities for students to engage in extracurricular activities, develop leadership skills, and connect with peers who share similar interests. From sports and cultural events to community service and academic research, our societies cater to a wide range of interests and passions.

Event Management Society

This society organizes various events, including workshops, seminars, and cultural festivals, promoting engagement and creativity among students.

Sports Society

The Sports Society encourages students to participate in various sports and physical activities, fostering teamwork and sportsmanship.

Literary & Debating Society

This society enhances students' public speaking and critical thinking skills through debates, discussions, and literary events.

Alumni Association Club

The Alumni Association fosters connections between current students and alumni, providing networking and mentorship opportunities.

Blood Donor Society

This society organizes blood donation drives and awareness campaigns, contributing to community health and welfare.

Qirat & Naat Society

Focused on Islamic recitations and poetry, this society promotes cultural and religious awareness through competitions and events.

Social Media Team

This team manages the university's social media presence, promoting events and student achievements through various platforms.

Performing Art Society

The Performing Art Society provides a platform for students to showcase their talents in acting, music, and dance through performances and workshops.

Environment Protection & CSR Society

This society raises awareness about environmental issues and engages in community service projects to promote sustainability.

Research Society

The Research Society encourages students to engage in research projects and provides support for academic publications and conferences.

Monthly Magazine/Gazette Society

This society publishes a monthly magazine featuring student articles, interviews, and campus news, fostering creativity and writing skills.

Entrepreneurship & Incubation Club

The club supports aspiring entrepreneurs by providing resources, mentorship, and networking opportunities to develop innovative business ideas.

Career Counseling & Placement Cell

This cell assists students with career guidance, job placements, and workshops to enhance employability skills.
